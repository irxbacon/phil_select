# Phil Select Survey - Google Form Setup

This guide shows you how to set up a Google Form to collect crew member survey responses that can be imported into your Phil Select application.

## Step 1: Create Your Survey Form

### Copy the Pre-built Form Template

1. **Get the Form**: Click this link â†’ [Phil Select Survey Template](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1Pl1dZGzldD2GEoLOU5L3ueivJoEmqcMXHUIYYuGDlyc/copy)
2. **Make Your Copy**: Google will ask you to make a copy - click "Make a copy"
3. **Rename Your Form**: Give it a name like "Crew 123 - Phil Select Survey"
4. **Customize** (Optional): Edit any questions or settings as needed

## Step 2: Get Your Google Spreadsheet Link

After copying the form, you need to get the link to the response spreadsheet:

1. **Open Your Form**: Go to your copied form
2. **Click "Responses" Tab**: At the top of the form editor
3. **Create Spreadsheet**: Click the green spreadsheet icon and select "Create a new spreadsheet"
4. **Open the Spreadsheet**: Click "View responses in Sheets" 
5. **Copy the URL**: In the spreadsheet, copy the full URL from your browser's address bar

### Important: Make the Spreadsheet Public

For the Phil Select application to read your responses:

1. **In Your Spreadsheet**: Click the "Share" button (top right)
2. **Change Access**: Click "Change to anyone with the link"
3. **Set Permission**: Choose "Viewer" 
4. **Copy Link**: Copy the shareable link

## Step 3: Use the Spreadsheet in Phil Select

### Add to Your Survey Page

1. **Go to Your Phil Select Application**: Open your survey page
2. **Find the Spreadsheet URL Field**: Look for where you can enter a Google Sheets URL
3. **Paste Your Link**: Use the public link from Step 2
4. **Save Settings**: Make sure to save your changes

### Test the Integration

1. **Submit a Test Response**: Fill out your Google Form once
2. **Check Phil Select**: Verify the response appears in your application
3. **Import Data**: Use the import function to bring responses into your crew data

## What's Included in the Form

Your copied form will have:

- **Personal Information**: Name, age, outdoor experience level
- **Program Ratings**: All 56 Philmont programs organized by category
- **Dropdown Ratings**: 0-20 scale (default: 10) for each program
- **Automatic Collection**: Responses go directly to your Google Spreadsheet

## Sharing Your Form

### Get the Survey Link

1. **In Your Form Editor**: Click the "Send" button
2. **Copy Link**: Use the link tab to get a shareable URL
3. **Share with Crew**: Send this link to your crew members

### Example Message to Crew

```
Please complete our crew survey by [date]:
[Your Google Form URL]

This helps us plan which activities to prioritize during our Philmont trek.
```

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

- **Can't Access Spreadsheet**: Make sure it's set to "Anyone with the link can view"
- **No Responses Showing**: Check that responses are going to the correct spreadsheet
- **Import Not Working**: Verify the spreadsheet URL is correct in Phil Select

### Getting Help

- **Google Forms Help**: Use Google's help documentation for form issues
- **Phil Select Support**: Contact your system administrator for import problems
